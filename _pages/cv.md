## Semantics for Indoor Scene Layout and Dataset Augmentation

Digital indoor scenes are complex data structures, and current datasets lack sufficient quantity and diversity to support the training of deep generative models. We aim to model semantics such as "object relationships" and "functional groups" in indoor scenes to expand the existing indoor scene datasets, enhancing their effectiveness in training deep generative models.
