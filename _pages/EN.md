In the acknowledgments of my master's thesis, after thanking my advisor and senior, I wrote the last paragraph as this:
>"Finally, I will not thank myself. Because this fledgling eagle has flown into my dreams many times. It sits by the lotus pond, what is it fantasizing about? Perhaps fantasizing about the sky being filled with delivery drones, perhaps fantasizing about every household having robots to take care of their lives, perhaps fantasizing about everyone being able to fly into the canyon with VR glasses to listen to the sound of destiny's breaking. I don't know, I can only stand quietly behind that eagle, afraid that it will turn around and see me and great to me. Just because that, at this moment, there are too many questions that cannot be answered, too many expectations that are difficult to fulfill, and too many doubts that cannot be responded to. I also don't want to make up reasons, I'll just agree to regrets with my silence. However, I'm grateful that the road ahead is not over, and I'll keep my head down and way up. Maybe, the answer to the questions, are still ahead..."

This paragraph exactly fits as the content of my personal homepage.
